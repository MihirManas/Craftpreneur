Interactive Course Modules

Click on each module to discover the practical skills and knowledge you will gain. Our curriculum is designed for immediate, real-world application.

Module 1: Crafting Your Business Foundation

Demystifying the legal, administrative, and foundational aspects of establishing a legitimate craft business.

  • ✓
    Actionable Steps: Learn step-by-step instructions on company registration (Proprietorship, LLP), obtaining GST registration, and understanding essential business licenses.
  • ✓
    Build Credibility: Navigate the process for NSDC certifications to build trust and access government schemes.

Module 2: Mastering the Art of Digital Showcase & Sales

Equipping you with the essential digital skills to beautifully present and effectively sell your handcrafted products online.

  • ✓
    Visual Storytelling: Master professional product photography (even with a smartphone) and craft compelling brand stories that sell.
  • ✓
    E-commerce Excellence: Set up and manage e-commerce platforms (Etsy, local marketplaces), develop effective social media strategies, and handle secure online payments.

Module 3: Global Reach - Logistics, Quality & Exports

Navigating the critical challenges of packaging, shipping, quality assurance, and international trade to access global markets.

  • ✓
    Secure Shipping: Learn best practices for packaging delicate crafts, and understand domestic and international shipping logistics.
  • ✓
    Go International: Get guidance on obtaining the crucial Export-Import Code (IEC), adhering to international quality standards, and basic customs procedures.
